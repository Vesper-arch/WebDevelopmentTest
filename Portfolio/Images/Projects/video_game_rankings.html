My Top 5 Games

  1. Celeste - ENOK Games - 2018
    • Excellent controls
    • Platforming is difficult but quite generous
    • Large speedrunning/TASing community
    • Trans Rights!
  2. Outer Wilds - Mobius Digital - 2019
    • Incredible story
    • Immersive and interesting mechanics
    • BOTW-esque exploration and discovery system
  3. Risk of Rain 2 - Hopoo Games - 2020
    • Difficulty scales with everything you do.
    • 186 unique items that can be stacked infinitely*
    • Thousands of mods + strong modding community
  4. Vampire Survivors(Steam page) - poncle - 2022
    • Simple controls; Just move.
    • Unique weapons and skills
    • Large amount of content
    • Has the ability to stress test your graphics card if you try hard enough
    • $9 bucks for the complete game.(without sales) Why wouldn't you buy it?
